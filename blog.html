Speak Like a Pro

September 19 2024

Public speaking is a valuable skill that benefits you across many areas of life! In both personal and professional life, better speaking skills open new avenues of growth. But despite its importance, many of us struggle with speaking skills. Do you know that public speaking is one of the greatest fears of most people?
Yes
A study shows that 75% of the students had a fear of public speaking and 95% of the participants agreed that instructions, counseling, and coaching can help to overcome this fear.
Not everyone indeed has great speaking skills in communication! But in the end, it is not a hopeless endeavor either!
The good news is that we can always improve speaking and enhance our opportunities for communication.

How to Motivate Students

October 7 2024

What is Motivation?
Motivation is a key determinant that drives learning!, It is a central aspect that influences success and a prominent reason behind an individual’s behavior.
What are the Benefits of Motivating Students?
Motivational learning improves a student’s overall readiness to learn. And eventually, it helps him/her to reach the milestones of academic success. When a teacher knows how to motivate the students in classroom, it promotes a positive learning environment. A good classroom environment and effective instruction help students to develop as lifelong learners. This means that they develop the ability to stay intrinsically motivated to learn in the long run.
How to Motivate Students to Learn?
Motivating a student can be a tough task for teachers/parents. Because many times, students do not know the real meaning of self motivation, and lack a drive to learn. In almost all cases, simple lifestyle changes and strategies can do wonders for a student’s motivation levels. The good news is that all children have the capability to learn, provided we know how to motivate them well!
